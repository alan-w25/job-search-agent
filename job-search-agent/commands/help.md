# Job Search Agent — Help

Here's everything available:

## Skills

| Skill | What it does | Try saying |
|:------|:-------------|:-----------|
| **career-profile-builder** | Build your career profile by interrogation or resume upload | "Build my profile" / "Parse my resume" |
| **job-analyzer** | Analyze a JD, score your match, decode hidden signals | "Analyze this JD" / "Am I qualified?" |
| **resume-tailor** | Generate ATS-optimized resume targeted to a specific JD | "Tailor my resume for this role" |
| **cover-letter-writer** | Generate targeted cover letters matched to JD and company culture | "Write a cover letter" / "Cover letter for this role" |
| **interview-coach** | Company research, interview prep, mock interviews, recruiter coaching, offer negotiation | "Prep me for my interview" / "The recruiter said X" |
| **networking-strategist** | Outreach messages, LinkedIn optimization, referral scripting, warm intro mapping | "Help me network" / "Who should I reach out to?" |
| **skills-gap-closer** | Learning paths, portfolio projects, certifications to close skill gaps | "How do I close this gap?" / "What should I learn?" |
| **search-strategy-auditor** | Analyze your job search pipeline health, targeting, and conversion rates | "Is my search strategy working?" / "Audit my search" |

## Recommended Pipeline

```
1. Build profile (/career-profile-builder)
2. Paste a JD (/job-analyzer)
3. Close skill gaps (/skills-gap-closer) — if gaps identified
4. Tailor resume (/resume-tailor)
5. Write cover letter (/cover-letter-writer)
6. Network into the company (/networking-strategist)
7. Apply (you do this)
8. Research + prep (/interview-coach)
9. Practice (/interview-coach → mock interview)
10. Negotiate offer (/interview-coach → offer mode)
11. Audit your search (/search-strategy-auditor) — periodically
```

Or just describe your situation and I'll route you to the right place.
