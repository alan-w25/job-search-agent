# Job Search Agent — Help

Here's everything available:

## Skills

| Skill | What it does | Try saying |
|:------|:-------------|:-----------|
| **career-profile-builder** | Build your career profile by interrogation or resume upload | "Build my profile" / "Parse my resume" |
| **job-analyzer** | Analyze a JD, score your match, decode hidden signals | "Analyze this JD" / "Am I qualified?" |
| **resume-tailor** | Generate ATS-optimized resume targeted to a specific JD | "Tailor my resume for this role" |
| **interview-coach** | Company research, interview prep, mock interviews, recruiter coaching, offer negotiation | "Prep me for my interview" / "The recruiter said X" |

| Command | What it does |

## Recommended Pipeline

```
1. Build profile (/career-profile-builder)
2. Paste a JD (/job-analyzer)
3. Tailor resume (/resume-tailor)
4. Apply (you do this)
5. Research + prep (/interview-coach)
6. Practice (/interview-coach → mock interview)
7. Negotiate offer (/interview-coach → offer mode)
```

Or just describe your situation and I'll route you to the right place.
